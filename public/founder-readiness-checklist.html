Founder readiness checklist

Is the business ready for its next serious conversation?

Use this checklist before a fundraising process, strategy reset, board review or major growth decision. Mark what is genuinely ready—not what you hope is ready.

01 · Strategy

We can state the customer, problem and differentiated position in one clear sentence.
We know which segments and opportunities are priorities—and what we are deliberately not pursuing.
Our next 90 days connect directly to a larger commercial goal.
Founder prompt: What belief about the market would most damage the plan if it proved false?

02 · Finance

Revenue, gross margin, operating cost and cash runway are visible and current.
Our forecast assumptions can be explained and defended.
We know how much capital is required, what it unlocks and when the next milestone is reached.
Founder prompt: Which single assumption creates the greatest swing in cash or valuation?

03 · Narrative

The story connects market opportunity, founder insight, traction, economics and the ask.
Every major claim has evidence behind it.
The founder team can deliver the story consistently without hiding the risks.
Founder prompt: What should an investor remember about the company twenty-four hours after the meeting?

04 · Readiness

Core legal, commercial and financial documents are organised for diligence.
The team knows the next decision, owner and deadline.
We can explain where external support is needed and what a successful engagement changes.
